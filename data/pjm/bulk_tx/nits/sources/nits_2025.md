Transcribed from PJM's published "Annual Transmission Revenue Requirements (ATRR)
and Network Integration Transmission Service (NITS) Rates" PDFs. Each PDF contains
one table with columns: Transmission Zone, Transmission Owner(s), Annual Revenue
Requirement, Total Zonal Annual Revenue Requirement, and Network Integration
Transmission Service Rate ($/MW-Year). Only the four MD-relevant zones are recorded
here. This file is the source of record for the matching rows in `nits_rates.csv`;
regenerate with `just -f data/pjm/bulk_tx/nits/Justfile convert`.

| zone  | zonal_peak_mw | date       | hour_ending_ept | season |

| PEPCO | 6161.7        | 2024-07-16 | 18              | summer |

The season column determines the PoP seasonal filter: BGE, DPL, and PEPCO all
peak in summer (Jun–Sep), while APS peaks in winter (Dec–Mar). This governs
which months' load curve drives the hourly allocation of annual NITS cost.

## Notes

- **PEPCO zone ATRR**: The PEPCO zone includes both Potomac Electric Power
  Company and SMECO as separate transmission owners. The Jun 2025 PDF shows
  SMECO ATRR = $17.1M separately; the combined zonal NITS rate already
  incorporates both TOs.
- **DPL zone ATRR**: The DPL zone includes Old Dominion Electric Cooperative
  (ODEC) as a second transmission owner ($5.8M ATRR at Jun 2025). The
  published NITS rate is the combined zonal rate.
- **APS zone**: The APS NITS rate did not change between Jan and Jun 2025.
  The same ATRR ($156.1M) applies to both periods.
- **Cross-reference**: All Jun 2025 rates independently confirmed via
  CAPS Transmission Handbook Vol. 6 (July 2025),
  `https://www.dgardiner.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/CAPS-Transmission-Handbook-Volume-6_Version-2-Updated-July-2025.pdf`.
