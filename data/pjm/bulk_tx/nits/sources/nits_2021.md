## Notes

- **APS zone**: Rate updates in January and remains constant through June.
- **BGE and DPL zones**: Rates update in June; January rates are carryforward
  from the previous year's June update.
- **PEPCO zone**: Rate changes at both January (NSPL reset) and June (new ATRR).
  The January 2021 rate of `$28,166` is a slight increase from 2020's rate due
  to NSPL adjustment, then the June 2021 rate of `$31,497` reflects the new
  ATRR filing.
