## Notes

- **APS zone**: New rate took effect in January (`$18,162`, up from `$13,930`
  in 2021) and remained constant through June.
- **BGE and DPL zones**: January rates carried forward from June 2021; new
  rates took effect in June 2022.
- **PEPCO zone**: January rate of `$31,525` represents a minor NSPL adjustment
  from the June 2021 rate of `$31,497`. June rate of `$37,201` reflects a
  significant ATRR increase (18% jump).
- The 2022 PDF also shows a September 2022 interim update, but the primary
  filing periods remain January and June.
