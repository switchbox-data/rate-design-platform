## Notes

- **APS zone**: New rate of `$16,760` took effect in January (down from
  `$18,162` in 2022) and remained constant through June.
- **BGE and DPL zones**: January rates carried forward from June 2022; new
  rates took effect in June 2023. DPL saw a significant 11.5% increase from
  `$49,476` to `$55,166`.
- **PEPCO zone**: January rate of `$37,201` carried forward with minimal NSPL
  adjustment from June 2022. June rate increased 14.7% to `$42,675`.
