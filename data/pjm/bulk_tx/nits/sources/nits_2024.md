## Notes

- **APS zone**: New rate of `$17,115` took effect in January (up from `$16,760`
  in 2023) and remained constant through June.
- **BGE and DPL zones**: January rates carried forward from June 2023; new
  rates took effect in June 2024. Both zones saw significant increases: BGE up
  20.4% to `$55,851`, DPL up 12.2% to `$61,897`.
- **PEPCO zone**: January rate of `$42,656` reflects an NSPL adjustment from
  the June 2023 rate of `$42,675`. June rate increased 28.5% to `$54,821`, the
  largest percentage jump among the four zones in 2024.
- **PEPCO ATRR for June 2024**: Not extracted from available sources; the rate
  value is confirmed from the ETCC historical table.
- 2024 marked a year of significant transmission cost increases across all MD
  zones, with PEPCO experiencing the steepest climb.
