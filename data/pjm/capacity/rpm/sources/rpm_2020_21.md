is the Base Residual Auction Resource Clearing Price for the zone's LDA (copied
to each member zone). `final_zonal_capacity_price_per_mw_day` is the Final Zonal
Capacity Price (IA-inclusive, per zone). `lda` is the most-specific LDA that
cleared separately for the zone this DY (RTO = system price). Zone labels are
normalized to canonical (see `data/pjm/README.md` crosswalk); UGI (inside PPL)
and OVEC are excluded. This file is the source of record for the matching rows
in `rpm_capacity_prices.csv`; regenerate with
`just -f data/pjm/capacity/rpm/Justfile convert`.
